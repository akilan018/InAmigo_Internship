About This Project

Why Project Prakriti?

India faces an environmental crisis — rising temperatures, depleted forests, choked rivers, and mountains of waste. Project Prakriti responds with grassroots action, mobilizing communities to protect, restore, and celebrate the natural world.

From massive tree plantation drives on World Environment Day to neighborhood clean-up campaigns, plastic-free awareness drives, and composting workshops — Prakriti turns environmental concern into tangible, community-led action.

We believe every sapling planted today is a gift to future generations. Even planting one tree in your garden can reduce local temperatures, improve air quality, and inspire others to act. Small steps, together, create a massive impact.

Our Objectives

Goals of Project Prakriti

Six pillars of our environmental action strategy.

01🌳

Mass Tree Plantation

Organize large-scale sapling plantation drives in urban neighborhoods, schools, roadsides, and public parks to increase green cover across cities and towns.

02🗑️

Waste Management

Drive awareness about segregation at source, composting of organic waste, and responsible disposal — reducing India's catastrophic waste burden community by community.

03🚯

Plastic-Free Campaigns

Conduct neighborhood clean-up drives and anti-plastic campaigns that remove single-use plastics from streets, rivers, and public spaces — replacing them with eco-friendly alternatives.

04💧

Water Conservation

Educate communities on rainwater harvesting, water-saving practices, and the importance of protecting local water bodies through workshops and demonstrations.

05🏫

School Eco-Clubs

Partner with local schools to establish eco-clubs where young students learn about climate change, biodiversity, and sustainability — building the next generation of environmental stewards.

06🌏

Climate Awareness

Organize public exhibitions, social media campaigns, and community discussions on climate change, deforestation, and India's environmental challenges — inspiring action at every level.

What We Do

Our Activities

Real-world actions that heal and protect our environment.

01

Tree Plantation Drives

Organized drives on World Environment Day, Independence Day, and other occasions where volunteers, communities, and corporates come together to plant hundreds of saplings in a single event.

02

Neighborhood Clean-Up Campaigns

Monthly clean-up drives in urban neighborhoods, parks, riverbanks, and roadsides — removing litter, separating waste, and transforming public spaces into cleaner, greener areas.

03

Plastic-Free Awareness Drives

Distribution of cloth bags, bamboo bottles, and biodegradable alternatives combined with door-to-door awareness campaigns educating households on the dangers of single-use plastics.

04

Composting & Kitchen Garden Workshops

Practical sessions teaching families to compost kitchen waste, create kitchen gardens, and reduce their household carbon footprint through simple, sustainable daily habits.

05

Sapling Care Follow-Up Programs

Post-plantation follow-up visits ensuring planted saplings are watered, protected, and nurtured by local residents — maximizing survival rates and long-term green impact.
