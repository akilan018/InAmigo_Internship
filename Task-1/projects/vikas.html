About This Project

Why Project Vikas?

India's youth unemployment crisis is real. Millions of graduates enter the job market every year, but too many lack the practical skills, professional exposure, and confidence that employers actually need. Project Vikas bridges this gap through structured, impactful internship programs.

As noted on our LinkedIn page: "InAmigos Foundation is not limited by borders." Students from across India and even internationally join Vikas — learning digital marketing, content creation, research, data analysis, HR, finance, and social work — while contributing to real NGO operations.

Every intern gains verified certification, LinkedIn recommendations, project experience, and a community of like-minded young leaders. Vikas doesn't just build careers — it builds character and a sense of social responsibility.

Our Objectives

Goals of Project Vikas

Comprehensive youth development across professional, social, and leadership dimensions.

01💻

Digital Skills Training

Provide hands-on training in digital marketing, social media management, SEO, content writing, graphic design, and web analytics — the most in-demand skills of the digital economy.

02🔬

Research & Analysis

Train interns in data collection, social research methodology, impact assessment, and report writing — building analytical thinking and evidence-based decision-making skills.

03💰

Finance & CSR Awareness

Introduce youth to NGO finance, grant writing, CSR proposal preparation, and impact reporting — preparing them for careers in the growing social impact sector.

04🗣️

Leadership & Communication

Build public speaking, team management, conflict resolution, and project leadership skills through structured workshops, presentations, and real-world team assignments.

05🤝

Social Work Exposure

Give interns direct exposure to ground-level NGO operations — field visits, beneficiary interactions, community mobilization — building empathy and social consciousness.

06🏅

Certification & Career Support

Issue verified certificates, provide LinkedIn recommendations, conduct resume workshops, and connect high-performing interns with placement and career development opportunities.

What We Do

Internship Tracks

Multiple specialized tracks to match your interests and career goals.

🎨

Digital Marketing & Content

Manage IAF's social media pages, create posts, design graphics, write blogs, and run awareness campaigns — gaining a portfolio of real, published work across Instagram, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp.

📊

Research & Documentation

Conduct community surveys, analyze impact data, write reports, and document project outcomes — building strong research and documentation skills valued by employers worldwide.

💼

Human Resources & Operations

Support volunteer recruitment, onboarding, scheduling, and coordination — gaining practical HR experience in a dynamic, mission-driven organizational environment.

🌍

Virtual Internship Program

100% online internship accessible to students from anywhere in India or the world — with structured tasks, weekly check-ins, mentor guidance, and a full certificate upon completion.

🏘️

Ground-Level Social Work

On-ground internship in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — participating directly in Bachpanshala sessions, Seva drives, Prakriti plantation events, and community development activities.

How to Apply

Application Process

Simple, transparent, and accessible — apply in 4 easy steps.

1️⃣

Apply Online

Fill out the internship application form on our website or reach us via LinkedIn/Instagram. Share your interests, skills, and preferred domain.

2️⃣

Selection & Onboarding

Our team reviews applications and selects interns based on interest and availability. Selected candidates receive onboarding materials and orientation within 3–5 days.

3️⃣

Work on Real Projects

Complete structured tasks assigned by your domain mentor — with weekly feedback, milestone check-ins, and access to IAF resources and platforms.

4️⃣

Certificate & Recommendation

Upon successful completion, receive a verified internship certificate, a LinkedIn recommendation from IAF leadership, and a testimonial for your portfolio.
