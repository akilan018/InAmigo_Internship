About This Project

Why Bachpanshala?

In India, millions of children from underprivileged backgrounds are robbed of their childhood by poverty, lack of resources, and systemic barriers. Project Bachpanshala was born from the belief that every child deserves a chance to learn, grow, and dream regardless of their socio-economic background.

Launched under the InAmigos Foundation in 2020, Bachpanshala runs community learning centers, digital literacy drives, and creative workshops in underserved neighborhoods. Our volunteers — trained educators and enthusiastic youth — conduct regular sessions that blend academics with life skills, arts, and emotional well-being.

From distributing books and stationery to setting up digital learning corners, we ensure children don't just attend school — they love learning. Because the strongest futures are built from the earliest stages of life.

Our Objectives

Goals of Bachpanshala

Six clear objectives that guide every session, every drive, and every interaction.

01 📖

Foundational Literacy

Ensure every child can read, write, and perform basic arithmetic through structured, engaging classroom-style sessions in community settings.

02 💻

Digital Literacy

Introduce children to computers, tablets, and the internet in a safe, guided way — building skills essential for the 21st century economy.

03 🎨

Creative Expression

Foster imagination and confidence through art, music, storytelling, and drama — nurturing the whole child, not just the student.

04 🧠

Life Skills Training

Teach critical thinking, decision-making, hygiene awareness, and social skills that empower children to navigate the world confidently.

05 📦

Resource Distribution

Provide free books, stationery, school bags, and educational materials to children who cannot afford basic learning supplies.

06 👨‍👩‍👧

Family Engagement

Involve parents and guardians through awareness sessions, encouraging a supportive home learning environment for sustained progress.

What We Do

Our Activities

Hands-on programs designed to make a real, measurable difference.

01

Community Learning Sessions

Weekly educational classes conducted in community halls, open spaces, and homes by trained volunteers covering Math, Science, Hindi, and English for children aged 5–16.

02

Book & Stationery Distribution Drives

Regular distribution events supplying notebooks, pens, pencils, school bags, and age-appropriate books to children across underserved colonies and slum areas.

03

Digital Awareness Workshops

Hands-on sessions introducing children to laptops, basic internet use, online safety, and educational platforms — bridging the digital divide one session at a time.

04

Festival Celebrations & Joy Days

Special events during festivals, Children's Day, and Republic Day that combine fun with learning — because happiness and curiosity go hand in hand.

05

Health & Hygiene Awareness

Age-appropriate sessions on personal hygiene, nutrition, and health habits — building healthy routines that last a lifetime.
