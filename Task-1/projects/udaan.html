Helping Women Soar to New Heights

Udaan — meaning "flight" — is InAmigos Foundation's mission to liberate women from economic dependence and social barriers. Through skill training, entrepreneurship support, and awareness campaigns, we empower women to build independent, dignified lives in both rural and urban India.

About This Project

Why Project Udaan?

Despite being half of India's population, millions of women remain economically dependent, socially marginalized, and stripped of decision-making power. Project Udaan was created to change this reality — one woman, one skill, one story at a time.

We run skill development workshops covering tailoring, embroidery, handicrafts, digital marketing, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship basics. Our trained facilitators visit communities, setup local learning hubs, and mentor women through their journey from learner to entrepreneur.

Beyond skills, Udaan also addresses menstrual hygiene awareness, self-defense training, legal rights education, and mental health support — because true empowerment means dignity in every aspect of life.

Our Objectives

Goals of Project Udaan

Holistic empowerment across economic, social, and personal dimensions.

01 🧵

Vocational Skills

Train women in market-relevant vocational skills — tailoring, handicrafts, beauty services, and food processing — enabling sustainable income generation.

02 💼

Entrepreneurship Support

Guide women to launch micro-enterprises through business planning workshops, market linkages, and access to microfinance and government schemes.

03 📱

Digital Financial Literacy

Teach women to use mobile banking, UPI payments, and government e-portals — bringing them into the formal financial ecosystem.

04 🌸

Health & Hygiene Awareness

Conduct menstrual hygiene workshops, distribute sanitary products, and destigmatize conversations around women's health in rural communities.

05 ⚖️

Legal Rights Education

Inform women about their legal rights, domestic violence laws, property rights, and available government support systems through awareness sessions.

06 🧘

Mental Wellness Support

Create safe spaces for women to share, heal, and grow — with peer support groups and access to mental health resources and counseling references.

What We Do

Our Activities

On-the-ground programs that create real, lasting change in women's lives.

01

Skill Development Workshops

Hands-on vocational training workshops in tailoring, embroidery, cooking, beauty, and handicraft — conducted at community centers and women's self-help group meetings.

02

Udaan Entrepreneurship Bootcamps

Intensive 2-day bootcamps teaching business registration, pricing, online selling, and branding — with post-bootcamp mentorship from industry professionals.

03

Menstrual Hygiene Drives

Free distribution of sanitary pads combined with educational sessions covering menstrual health, hygiene practices, and busting common myths in rural communities.

04

Self-Defense Training

Basic self-defense sessions conducted in partnership with trained instructors, building physical confidence and personal safety awareness among women and girls.

05

Women's Day Celebrations

Annual events honoring women's achievements, featuring motivational speakers, talent showcases, recognition ceremonies, and networking opportunities for local women entrepreneurs.
