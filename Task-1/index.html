Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO licensed by the Central Government of India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

At InAmigos Foundation, we are committed to fostering community development and social empowerment through impactful, sustainable initiatives. We strive to bridge gaps in education, healthcare, and economic opportunities for underprivileged communities.

Our mission is built on the principles of inclusivity, empathy, and collaboration, addressing the diverse needs of the communities we serve. By working hand-in-hand with local leaders, volunteers, and partner organizations, we design culturally sensitive, community-driven solutions that inspire lasting impact.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society where every individual has access to opportunities, dignity, and growth — regardless of background, geography, or circumstance.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, women empowerment, animal welfare, and youth skill development — creating a ripple of lasting social change across India.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity & Empathy
  • Transparency & Integrity
  • Collaboration & Innovation
  • Grassroots Engagement

Social Impact

Why Our Work Matters

Every initiative creates a ripple of change that transforms communities and builds brighter futures.

Education for All

When children learn, communities grow. Project Bachpanshala ensures no child is left behind — nurturing creativity, confidence, and curiosity in underprivileged kids across India.

Women Rising

Empowered women build stronger families and communities. Project Udaan breaks barriers, enabling women to achieve financial independence, entrepreneurship, and dignity.

Greener Tomorrow

Every sapling planted today is a gift to future generations. Project Prakriti's tree drives and eco-awareness campaigns combat climate change at the grassroots level.

Hunger-Free Lives

No one should sleep hungry. Project Seva's volunteers step forward every day — rain or shine — to feed, clothe, and support those in greatest need.

Voice for the Voiceless

Animals deserve care and protection. Project Jeev rescues, feeds, and cares for stray animals — turning compassion into action for India's most vulnerable creatures.

Youth-Led Future

Project Vikas equips young people with real-world skills through internships and training programs — building a generation of changemakers ready to lead India forward.
