# 🎨 NGO Website Feature Highlights
### InAmigos Foundation — Task 4: Figma Design Task

---

## 📌 About This Project

This project was completed as **Task 4** of the **InAmigos Foundation** internship programme.

The objective was to identify key features that every NGO website should have and present them visually as a **Figma design layout** — with clear labels and notes explaining each feature's purpose and real-world importance.

> **Design Tool:** Figma
> **Export Format:** PDF (11 pages)
> **Features Covered:** 6 key NGO website sections

## 🎯 Features Designed (6 Key Sections)

### 01 · 🏠 Hero Banner + Donation Section
- Full navigation bar with highlighted **Donate Now** CTA
- Bold mission headline with supporting subtext
- Embedded **donation widget** with preset amounts (₹200 / ₹500 / ₹1000 / ₹2000 / ₹5000 / Custom)
- UPI / Card / Net Banking payment options
- 80G tax exemption + SSL trust badges
- Statistics bar — volunteers, funds raised, communities, states covered

> 💡 *The hero is the first thing a visitor sees. An embedded donation card removes all friction — visitors can give without navigating away.*

---

### 02 · 📊 Impact Showcase
- 6 color-coded impact metric cards — Education, Health, Environment, Employment, Food, Volunteers
- Key numbers: 8,200 students · 3,540 health camps · 42,000 trees · 12,400 volunteers
- Annual goals **progress bars** with percentage fills
- Each cause area has a distinct color theme for quick scanning

> 💡 *Donors want proof before they commit. Transparent impact numbers build trust and drive recurring support.*

---

### 03 · 🙋 Volunteer Registration Form
- Name, email, phone, city input fields
- Area of interest selector
- **Skill tag chips** — Teaching, Web Design, Photography, Content Writing, Social Media, etc.
- Availability picker
- Privacy statement and security badge
- Auto-confirmation email on submit

> 💡 *A structured form collects the right data to match volunteers to the right cause — reducing coordinator workload and sign-up drop-offs.*

---

### 04 · 💬 Testimonials & Stories
- 3 testimonial cards — Student Volunteer, Corporate CSR Partner, Monthly Donor
- Star ratings with italic quote styling
- **Featured beneficiary story** — full-width dark banner with photo and quote
- Authentic voices from across the community

> 💡 *Social proof is the most powerful driver of first-time donations and volunteer sign-ups. People trust people, not organizations.*

---

### 05 · 🎯 Active Campaigns
- 3 campaign cards — Digital Classrooms, Rural Health Camps, Tree Plantation Drive
- Status badges: **ACTIVE** · **URGENT** · **ONGOING**
- Real-time **fundraising progress bars** with goal vs raised amounts
- Days remaining + donor count display
- Individual campaign donate buttons

> 💡 *Specific goals + visible progress bars tap into completion psychology — campaign pages raise 3× more than generic donate buttons.*

---

### 06 · ✉ Newsletter Signup + Contact
- Newsletter form with **interest tag checkboxes** (Education, Volunteering, Health, Campaigns)
- Subscriber count trust signal (8,400+ subscribers)
- Unsubscribe assurance copy
- 3 contact cards — Email · Phone · Office Address

> 💡 *Email capture turns one-time visitors into long-term community members. Segmented newsletters keep supporters engaged between campaigns.*

## 💡 Top 3 Recommendations for InAmigos Website

Based on this design research, here are the highest-impact features to implement first:

1. **Donation Widget on Homepage** — Highest ROI. Even a simple embed with 3–4 preset amounts will significantly increase online giving.
2. **Impact Numbers Section** — Easy to implement, massive trust builder. Update numbers quarterly.
3. **Volunteer Form with Skill Tags** — Replace generic contact forms with a structured signup that captures data coordinators actually need.
